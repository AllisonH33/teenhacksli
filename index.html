Call for Organizers!

Do you want to help organize the next TeenHacks LI? Then, Apply now!

In Spring 2019, we plan on having a bigger and better event with 250+ participants. We need you to help us make this possible.

We need help with everything such as: student outreach, media relations, sponsorship outeach, speaker/mentor recruitment, general logistics, marketing, web design, and much more.

We're looking for:

  • Students passionate about CS and hackathons.
  • Organizers diligent and highly motivated ready to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • Leaders who want to build a hacker community on Long Island.

Eligibility

  • Must be current high school student (preferably in Long Island, NY)
  • Must have attended at least one hackathon
  • Preferred: prior experience in organizing events/CS club
