About Us

TeenHacks LI is Long Island's premier 24-hour student hackathon, for high schoolers, by high schoolers. We are excited to build the hacker community, giving students the opportunity to meet leaders in tech, mentors, and peers. We will have workshops and talks for coders of all levels, along with prizes.

FAQs

What is a hackaton?

It's a place where programmers, engineers, designers, and entrepreneurs come together to build an awesome project in 24-hours. We provide the venue and food, you bring the creativity. Get ready to learn, build something cool, and meet new people. Check out this article by Dave Fontenot to learn more! What is a Hackathon?

What happens if I’m a beginner?

Do not worry. TeenHacks LI encourages students of all levels to come! In addition, we will have workshops throughout the hackathon as well as many mentors and teachers walking around to answer any of your questions.

Who can attend?

All high school students are welcome to attend. If you're 19 or over, shoot us an email and let us know. College students are welcomed to sign-up as mentors or volunteers!

Does it cost anything?

TeenHacks LI is completely free, thanks to our generous sponsors! Food is included in registration.

What if I don’t have a team?

Don’t worry, we have you covered. In the beginning, we’ll have a team formation session to make sure you’re not alone. Only teams of 2-4 will be eligible for prizes.

What should I bring?

You should bring your student ID, laptop, phone, chargers, sleeping bag, toothbrush, as well as anything you may need for your project.

Where do I sleep?

We encourage everyone to sleep for at least a portion of the night. You can bring a sleeping bag and pillow. We will have a secure quiet room for those who want some shut eye.
